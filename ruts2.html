STEP 1

Log in to your account through your registered user ID, password if dont have account create it by clicking on register button

STEP 2

Once login then click on book ticket then enter your origin and destination stations under the From-To option, date of journey and preferred train name and number for travel

STEP 3

Once you entered your details click on book ticket and wait for some time

STEP 4

Once the process will get finished on screen you will see your ticket is booked and will receive one ticket number

FINISH

If you want to view the ticket you can click on view ticket button and see the ticket.
